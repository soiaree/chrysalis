Let's keep it simple.

How would you describe your style in one word? Fashion and style is not only a means to express who you are, but also who you aspire to be. Crysalis can help you get to where you want to be through our stylists who find you sustainably-sourced outfits.

VALUES

Let’s admit it-- clothes can have a huge impact on how we act. We want to dress you in the right clothes so your lifestyle is transformed for the better. Along the way, we aim to give a stage to designers and source from ethical brands and shops owned by minorities and women.

THE PROBLEMS WE ARE TACKLING

Fast fashion and its effects on the environment

  • Fast fashion is second only to oil as the world’s largest polluter.
  • In the US, people spend 1,833 on clothes a year only to wear 25% of it.
  • We buy 2 times more clothes than we did 15 years ago

Rampant racism in the fashion industry

  • In light of the BLM movement, various fashion brands were challenged to confront racist attitudes.
  • To create positive change, we believe it is imperative for us to support brands owned by minorities.

OUR STYLISTS

Our stylists are from some of the best design schools this country has, including the Cornell FSAD program, FIT, Parsons, and more. We enroll them in a training program to ensure they can give you the best possible guidance.
